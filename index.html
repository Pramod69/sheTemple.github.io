Who We Are

We are a fashion house dealing exclusively in bespoke clothing.

Our brand has been in the line of exquisite customization of aspired outfits that are handcrafted to perfection since the 1990’s.
We at She Temple look forward to giving each outfit a personal and unique touch, something that originally belongs to you. Those distinguishing features that make your clothing unique and special.
Bespoke clothing therefore helps in defining a person’s unique style, unique taste, unique body and comfort level.
We specialize in hand embroidery, stitching and personalized designing and love to keep the Indian unique hand karigiri/embroideries alive, whatever the type of embroidery we can do it all. We have a highly skilled team with decades of experience with us in making dream attires a reality!
We are a brand that strongly believes, Fashion changes but style is personal and permanent. Every human finds and defines beauty differently and all of us are glamourous in our own way be it size, shape or color and therefore clothes should fit us rather than we fitting into them.

Contact Information

You can block an appointment two working days prior and have a face-to-face design consultation at the studio or a virtual video call design consultation during the appointment slot.

Please note we do not have any fixed prices and they differ from outfit to outfit based on the detailing, embroidery and other specifications required. Our stitching prices start from INR 2100, and hand embroideries from INR 5100.

On large we do not source fabrics, unless it is for a saree blouse, dupatta, tops or bottoms and or bridal lehengas from scratch. We under take in house dyeing as well if needed to match customization requirements.

We specialize in hand embroideries for bridal, occasion, festive, office and daily wear.

We would need anywhere from 2 weeks to 4 weeks to process and complete an order. However, we do take in special requests and deliver anything ranging from bridals to simple daily outfits within a week too, details of the same can be discussed during the appointment..
